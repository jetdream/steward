Two hundred and fourteen days. That's how long Biscuit — a nine-year-old beagle with gray around her muzzle — waited for a family. This Saturday, at our adoption day in Zilker Park, she climbed into a stranger's lap and stayed there.

She wasn't the only one. Four senior dogs found homes this weekend, part of a spring that has quietly broken every record our small rescue has kept.
