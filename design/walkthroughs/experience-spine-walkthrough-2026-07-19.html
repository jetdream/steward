01 · The soul

Five principles

One home, no navigation taxThe founder lives in a single surface and summons everything else. There are no destinations to learn.
The colleague speaks firstThe home is a conversation the system leads (CHT-5). The founder answers and disposes — never authors from blank.
The home morphs with trustDense and conversational early; sparse and report-like at earned autonomy. One invariant skeleton — density changes, layout never does.
Finite by designThe stream always reaches an honest "caught up". Holds and failures pin — never batch-cleared, never scrolled away. Safe at the typical ~1 item/day cadence (typical, not a cap — DEC-20).
The glass wall is always openKnowledge · How I write · Plan & Published · Discoveries — one click, plain labels, pull-only, never gated behind chat (VAL-3).

04 · The glass wall

Transparency without a dashboard

Knowledge

Facts, stories, people, rules — provenance on tap, AssumedNotes on inferences, edit/delete in place. Hosts the "help me understand you" open questions (INT-4).

How I write

The five-section editorial contract, versioned with diffs. Chat-made rules land here, marked with their origin. Binds immediately.

Plan & Published

The rolling four-week calendar over the publish log — one log, everywhere. Recall scheduled items; unpublish news articles.

Discoveries

Read-first triage (DEC-20): content worth reading leads; then one tap — worth a post (event-tied = time-sensitive), save for later (the planner's reserve for quiet weeks), not for us. No counts, no tasks, ever.

05 · The guarantees

Disciplines that keep the model honest

  • The stream always reaches an honest "caught up" — a fused home is safe only because it stays finite (typical ~1 item/day — typical, not a cap, DEC-20; bursts compress to packages).
  • Holds and failures pin. A GR-3 sensitive-topic draft interrupts at every trust level — counted, unmissable, never batch-approved.
  • One skeleton. The chrome and region order are identical across all shapes — the home never "changes on" the founder.
  • The glass wall never hides. Four labeled views, one click, in every shape; trust offers arrive with the evidence attached.
  • Never a blank box. The composer always suggests specific, non-trivial openings (CHT-5); disposals invite one optional "why" (the enrichment loop).
  • Rhythm, never streaks. "6 weeks of steady presence" — grace over guilt, one nudge maximum, warm welcome-backs (DEC-16).
